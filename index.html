About me

I first stepped into the programming world when I was just a kid: I saw my sister doing her homeworks, writing strange words on the computer, and I had to understand everything about those magic lines. This is how I learned playing with Python... Seriously, it looked like a game to me, but soon I figured out how powerful that programming language really was. The following Christmas my parents gave me a book about HTML5, and so I started making my first web pages and learning the basics of CSS and JavaScript too.
Now, developing my ideas is my favourite hobby! I love starting new projects and filling them with challenges that gradually increase my knowledge and my abilities. I see myself as a good full-stack developer who never stops learning: when I don't know how to make something, I'm really good at googling it.
Lately, I started to develop my interest about cybersecurity and IoT (I like to experiment with my Raspberry Pis) and I easily get excited about any new C.S. branches I discover while randomly surfing the Internet, looking for some solutions or just for fun!
